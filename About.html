ABOUT GAME!

Here at GAME!, we love games. We love games so much that we decided to devote our lives to give games to you. We do more than just give games to you. We make getting games from us such a pleasant experience that it will be hard to get from anywhere else(except Steam since they do their own thing. Steam's cool).

The idea for this website started around 15 years ago when our lead gamer Ken got frustrated when he went with his parents to buy a game at Blockbuster(back when they were still selling their exclusive but terrible quality games). Quality of the games aside, the pricing was atrocious and the purchasing experience was one of the worst he has ever experienced in his life. Even as a 5 year old kid, he could tell that the cashier lady was quite mean and inflexible. Ever since then, he decided that he would somehow find a way to defeat the Blockbuster overlords and acquire games in a pleasant and reasonable ways. When Blockbuster died on its own(a.k.a they didn't buy Netflix) Ken rejoiced with a laugh that would even be heard by species from a different galaxy. However, he still had one more mission and that is to make the world have a better game purchasing experience. Over the years he has met other people that share the same ambition as him and with their own terrible Blockbuster experiences to boot(Netflix FTW).
