Product description

The Nintendo Switch is the seventh major home video game console developed by Nintendo. Known in development by its codename NX,
it was unveiled in October 2016 and was released worldwide on March 3, 2017.
Nintendo considers the Switch a "hybrid" console; it is designed primarily as a home console, with the main unit inserted onto a docking station to connect to a television.
