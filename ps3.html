Product description

The PlayStation 3 (PS3) is a home video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the successor to PlayStation 2,
and is part of the PlayStation brand of consoles. It was first released on November 11, 2006, in Japan,[8] November 17, 2006, in North America,
and March 23, 2007, in Europe and Australia.[9][10][11] The PlayStation 3 mainly competes against consoles such as Microsoft's Xbox 360 and
Nintendo's Wii as part of the seventh generation of video game consoles.

  • Media : 550 MHz NVIDIA/SCEI RSX Reality Synthesizer
